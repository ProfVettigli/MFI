🏛️ CLIL: Architecture

Classical temples, Renaissance perspective, and Parametric algorithms.

1. The Classical Heritage

Greek architecture heavily relied on geometric harmony, specifically the Golden Ratio (circa 1.618), to scale temples like the Parthenon. Later, the Renaissance brought Linear Perspective into cupolas.

2. Modern Algorithmic Design

Today, architects utilize advanced Parametric Computer Scripts. By changing pure math numbers, the entire organic, curved 3D model transforms autonomously without drawing a single line manually.
